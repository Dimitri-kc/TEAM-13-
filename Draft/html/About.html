Frequently Asked Questions:

How can I place an order?
To place an order, select a product from a category and add it to your basket or click checkout, you will be redirected to the checkout page where you can continue as a guest, sign up or sign in. Finally, you can enter your payment information and confirm the payment.
How do I create a customer account?
To create an account, click on the my account icon which is in the shape of a human. Click on sign up and enter your details and click on create account.
Can I rate and review products I bought?
Yes all customers who have purchased a product can leave a review and anyone can view the reviews on a product.
How can I update my account information?
To update your account information, click on my account which is a human icon and click on my account settings. You can change your name, email address and password.
How can I get help if I have an issue or a question?
Click on the contact us page where you can enter your questions and report issues.
